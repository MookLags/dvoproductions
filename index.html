About

I'm Doug Leonard, a guy who's worn a lot of hats (and headsets). I went from the U.S. Navy to running live sound to hauling freight as a truck driver. Along the way, I learned discipline, grit, and the fine art of surviving both Navy inspections and truck stop coffee, which should honestly come with hazard pay. Every twist in that road taught me connection, resilience, and storytelling.

Now, I bring all of that to voiceover. Whether it's powering up a commercial or voicing a brand's story, I deliver with heart, humor, and authenticity. From Navy grit to studio wit, let's bring your story to life.
